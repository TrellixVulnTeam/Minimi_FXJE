Preschool Graduation 2018

Congratulations to our preschoolers from both Auburn and Campsie centres!

21 November 2018

The Graduation Ceremony was held on Saturday 10th November 2018 at the Parish Hall in Campsie. We are honoured to have all the families joined us to celebrate one of the important moments in our minimions’ life. Our Minimions have practiced for weeks for the performances. They were eager to show everyone what they had learnt and prepared for the day.

As for the year 2018, we started our Second Language Program and taught Korean as a second language in the Preschool rooms, the graduates chose their favourite Korean song and dance to perform. Also, our educators and Minimions who have a Chinese cultural background shared their culture with us. The preschoolers had chance to learn Chinese during the year. On the ceremony, the graduates performed a Chinese-Korean song and dance to show the families what they had learnt.

The foods and drinks were provided by our families, educators and the Minimi Café. The Minimi Café had a grand opening on the 26th September 2018. Since it opened, minimions had a lot of fun playing in it. They learnt to make cookies, muffins and sandwiches to sell in the café. Also, they learnt to line up to make order, serve customer, and negotiate conflicts during the play. For the Graduation Ceremony, the Minimi Café had prepared mininion cookies and mini muffins. All made by the minimions during the week before the ceremony.

The Graduation Ceremony is a great success and it is one of the most memorable moments in 2018. We appreciate our families’, educators’, and children’s support and contributions. We wish all the best for the Graduates’ future endeavors!
