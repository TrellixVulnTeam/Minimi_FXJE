Welcome TO Minimi Academy

Our aim and continual goal is to Provide the highest quality education and Childcare for children 0-6.


Our 75 place childcare centres are located in the Auburn and Campsie areas, catering for children ages 0-6 from 7am to 6pm. Our childcare centres have been carefully designed using a combination of research and insight from architects, families and early childhood professionals, assisting in establishing our vision and philosophy in providing the highest quality care in a safe and secure environment.


Our Child Care service aims to capture a home away from home feel that has environments that we hope will enable children to connect their home life to our Centre and our Centre to home. We want children to feel comfortable and respected each and every day they arrive to care. Each day your children will involve us in their unique conversations, journeys and ideas. With the mentoring and educational insight from our Educators, we make every effort to work in unison not only with your children, but with you.
